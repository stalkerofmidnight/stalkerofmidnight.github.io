About screens

This application was developed at the initial stage in the Android version. An application with a very large semantic and working language. It allows you to easily create your convenient digital mini store with your goods and contains the logic of 1C. The application has implemented a quick and easy-to-use sale and the addition of any of your products, it is possible to add sellers, the ability to track transactions and profits, as well as automatic support for current content. In fact, Ola is an application that takes care of your store for you and makes your life easier.

Scrum was used as a development methodology in this project. The work was promoted by steps determined by the whole team, decisions were made while coordinating all the nuances with each other, we were not afraid to experiment and put a lot of effort into working on the project following the rules of Scrum.
